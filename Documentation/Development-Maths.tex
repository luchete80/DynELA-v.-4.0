\section{The Tensor2 library}

The Tensor2 library is used to store second order tensors defined in the \DynELA.

A second order tensor is a like a matrix with the following form:
\begin{equation}
T=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
  T_{11} & T_{12} & T_{13}\\
  T_{21} & T_{22} & T_{23}\\
  T_{31} & T_{32} & T_{33}
  \end{array}\right]
\end{equation}
